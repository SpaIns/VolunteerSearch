About Us

VolunteerSearch is a platform to enhance the way you can connect with local organizations for volunteer opportunities that will broaden your skillsets. There are many of you willing to take time out of your lives to give time to others and our team at VolunteerSearch wants to make it possible for every volunteer to be able to do what they love. Our organizations are in demand of apt individuals that are committed to improving their community. To make the experience of volunteering search more favorable, a team of six first-time web developers came together to build this platform for individuals like you who love to help!
